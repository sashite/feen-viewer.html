About FEEN

FEEN (Field Expression Encoding Notation) is a minimalistic, rule-agnostic textual format for capturing static board positions in a canonical, compact, and game-neutral way.

FEEN strings consist of three space-separated fields: <PIECE-PLACEMENT> (board configuration), <HANDS> (pieces in each player's hand), and <STYLE-TURN> (styles and active player). Piece identifiers follow PIN-style conventions, with optional state modifiers (+, -) and optional terminal markers (^).
